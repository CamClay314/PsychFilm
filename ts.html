Overview

*The Shining* is a psychological horror film directed by Stanley Kubrick, based on Stephen King's novel. It tells the story of Jack Torrance, a writer who becomes the winter caretaker of the isolated Overlook Hotel.

Main Characters

  • Jack Torrance: A writer and the protagonist who descends into madness.
  • Wendy Torrance: Jack's wife, who tries to protect her son.
  • Danny Torrance: Jack's son, who has psychic abilities known as "The Shining."
